CULTURE vs TRADITION

Are "Culture" and "Tradition" terms that we can use interchangeably? No, they are not.

Although they are both concepts which define communities, thier beliefs, thier purposes are different and it pays excatly which is which.

Culture

Culture is a complex that incorporates the combination of a people's beliefs, Knowledge and traditions.

Tradition

Tradition consists in the passing down of beliefs from one generation to another generation.
